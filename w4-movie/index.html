The Lord of The Rings is a trilogy of films which brings J. R. R. Tolkien's novels to the big screen. The Fellowship of the Ring starts this journey. This story takes place in a fantasy world called Middle Earth. Ancient evil has returned to Middle Earth to regain his power and end the era of mankind. It is up to the people of Middle Earth to unite and stop this devilry.
